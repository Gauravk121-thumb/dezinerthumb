Refund Policy

We maintain a strict No Refund Policy once the work has commenced.

Due to the nature of digital design work, time and resources are allocated immediately upon booking. Therefore, the 50% advance payment is non-refundable.

We offer revisions as per the selected package to ensure satisfaction, but cancellations after work start are not eligible for refunds.

Terms & Conditions

1. Payments: A 50% deposit is required before starting any project. The remaining 50% is due upon completion but before the final delivery of high-quality files without watermarks.

2. Revisions: Standard packages include 2 revisions. Additional revisions are charged at ₹50 per iteration.

3. Files: We deliver PNG/JPG formats via email. Raw PSD files are NOT included in standard packages unless specified in the Enterprise/Pro plan.

4. Rights: Upon full payment, the client receives rights to use the designs for their intended purpose. Deziner reserves the right to display the work in portfolios.

Privacy Policy

We only collect information necessary to complete your project (Email, Name, Social Handles). Your creative assets and data are never shared with third parties.
